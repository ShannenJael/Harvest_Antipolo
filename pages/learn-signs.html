Learn Sign Language

Growing communication, fellowship, and Gospel outreach through practical lessons.

Learn Sign Language at HBC Antipolo

This page is designed for our church family and guests who want to learn sign language in a simple, Bible-centered way. These lessons can help us communicate more clearly, welcome more people warmly, and serve with greater compassion.

You can use the videos below for personal study, ministry practice, and review during the week. Each lesson loads only when clicked so the page stays fast even with many videos.

Who Can Join

Teens, adults, volunteers, and anyone willing to learn.

What You Will Practice

Vocabulary, conversation, worship terms, and ministry use.

Learn at Your Pace

Review the lessons anytime and return to them as needed.

Why It Matters

Every new skill becomes another way to show Christ's love.

Ready to Start?

If you want to join, ask questions, or use these lessons in ministry, we would love to help.

  • Use the lessons for weekly practice
  • Share the page with ministry workers
  • Reach out if you want guided instruction

Video Lessons

Tap any lesson below to load the video. Add your MP4 files to /videos/learn-signs/ and keep the filenames below, or update the file list in this page if your filenames differ.

Current lessons include religious signs, national signs, emotion signs, countries, animals, days of the week, and Bible names.
