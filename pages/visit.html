Plan Your Visit

We can't wait to meet you this Sunday!

You're Invited!

Your first visit to Harvest Baptist Church Antipolo will be refreshing, friendly, and enjoyable.

Whether you're new to the area, looking for a church home, or just visiting, we want you to feel welcome. At Harvest, you'll experience inspiring music, helpful Bible teaching and preaching, friendly people, and a place where families grow in faith together.

What to Expect

Worship & Music

Experience heartfelt worship with traditional hymns and uplifting music that honors God and encourages your soul.

Bible Teaching

Our services feature clear, practical Bible teaching that applies God's Word to everyday life.

Friendly People

You'll be greeted by friendly faces who are genuinely glad you're here. We're a family, and we want you to feel at home.

Deaf

Services interpreted for the Deaf, plus Deaf Bible Study.

Nursery

We have a safe and secure environment and wonderful staff to watch your children.

Children's Programs

Safe, fun, and engaging programs for children of all ages. Your kids will love it here!
